Canadian Housing Market Trends 2020-2025

Interprovincial migration represents the movement of people from one province or territory to another. Internal migration can be broken down into two components: interprovincial migration which is composed of movements of people from census division from one province or territory to census division of another province or territory and intraprovincial migration which is the movement of people from one census division to another within the same province or territory. Between 2016/2017 and 2018/2019, Canada saw significant shifts in the migration patterns between its provinces and territories. This migration has had varying impacts across the country, with some areas experiencing growth while others faced losses. Let's dive into the story behind the numbers and explore where people are moving and why it matters.
